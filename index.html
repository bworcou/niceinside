RETURNS


All online sales received through our website and instagram are final, itsniceinside does not offer returns or refunds once payment has been received. all pieces are sold as-is, we strive to describe the wear on each item as best as possible and urge people to physically inspect items before purchasing. some of the items we source can be over fifty years old, so understand they are not in new condition. pieces are priced accordingly to their wear so please take this into consideration. please measure accurately before purchasing an item to ensure it is correct for your space :~)



PICK-UP


Sold items must be picked up within 7 days of purchase. pick up is available via appointment from our abbotsford showroom. storage of more than seven days will incur a fee dependant on the square meterage of the purchased item, please contact us for a quote. if an item is not picked up after 30 days without prior agreements we reserve the right to re-list the item.



LOCAL DELIVERY


Delivery and interstate shipping are not calculated at the checkout. we offer delivery within a 50km radius of narrm/melbourne cbd. please feel free to contact us for a quote.



SHIPPING


Interstate shipping can be organised by the buyer, itsniceinside has no liability for damages incurred through interstate shipping. we are here to help facilitate delivery though are not held responsible for any mishaps, we urge customers to use couriers with insurance.

SELL WITH US



if you would like to consign an item with us enquire below by attaching photos of the item/s you would like to sell, with a brief description including the following information: brand, makers mark or tag (if applicable) condition, please note and photograph any wear age (if known) location based on the information provided, you will be notified within 2 business days whether the item/s will be consigned. if so we will make arrangments to inspect the item/s and arrange appropriate pick up and storage. once the sale price is agreed upon, you will be asked to sign a consignment agreement defining the agreed terms and conditions and the item/s will be listed online. once an item is picked up by us and taken into our care we will do an appraisal, take photos, provide storage and offer delivery of the item upon sale. once a consigned item is purchased you will be notified within 2 business days and a payment will be made to you within 7 days.
